Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is an open-ended country-life RPG! You've inherited your grandfather's old farm plot in Stardew Valley. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life. With over 50+ hours of gameplay content and new Mobile-specific features, such as auto-save and multiple controls options. Can you learn to live off the land and turn these overgrown fields into a thriving home?

  • Turn your overgrown fields into a lively and bountiful farm
  • Raise and breed happy animals, grow a variety of seasonal crops and design your farm, your way
  • Settle down and start a family with 12 potential marriage candidates
  • Become part of the community by taking part in seasonal festivals and villager quests
  • Explore vast, mysterious caves, encountering dangerous monsters and valuable treasure
